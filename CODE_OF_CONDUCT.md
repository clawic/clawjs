# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, direct, and technically honest. Disagreement is expected; contempt is not.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Deliberate bad-faith review or misrepresentation of someone else's work
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct. Report issues through the security/contact channels documented in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
